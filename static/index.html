ABOUT

Lys is a model based out of Sonoma County, California. Inspired by the beauty of California, she began her modeling career working by the seashore of San Fransisco and has been in love with it ever since.

She has been featured in magazines, has done campaigns, and worked with companies including Alli Blu Prom & Bridal, Melody White Photography, Starlet Prom & Bridal, and Team Whiskey, to name a few.
